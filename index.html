Discover Ethiopia

Explore the rich history, natural beauty, and cultural treasures of Ethiopia.

Lalibela

Famous for its 12th-century rock-hewn churches, Lalibela is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a spiritual heart of Ethiopia.

Simien Mountains

Known as the “Roof of Africa,” the Simien Mountains feature dramatic landscapes and rare wildlife like the Gelada baboon.

Blue Nile Falls

Called Tis Issat, or “The Water That Smokes,” the Blue Nile Falls are among the most breathtaking natural wonders in East Africa.

Aksum

Once the center of an ancient empire, Aksum is renowned for its towering obelisks and legends of the Ark of the Covenant.
